A demonstration of using Telescope in Neovim for fuzzy file finding and navigation.

## What is Shown

- Opening Neovim
- Launching Telescope with Space+ff
- Typing to filter files
- Selecting and opening a file

## Key Takeaway

Telescope brings the power of fzf into Neovim. Find and open files without leaving your editor.
