The content on this site is primarily AI-generated, but it documents dotfiles I've spent over a decade hand-curating. I've always wanted to create this documentation site but never took the time until AI made it possible.

## My Approach

**AI as a multiplier, not a replacement.** These dotfiles represent years of careful tuning and personal preference. AI helps me document them, but the configurations themselves are the result of a decade of refinement.

## Tools I Use

### Code Assistants

- **opencode** - AI agent in my terminal for development tasks
- **GitHub Copilot** - Inline code suggestions in my editor
- **ChatGPT** - For brainstorming, debugging, and research

### Writing

- **AI assistance** - For generating documentation from my configs
- **Grammar/spell check** - AI-enhanced proofreading

### Image Generation

- Occasionally for diagrams and illustrations when needed

## Principles

1. **Hand-curated configs** - The dotfiles are mine, refined over 10+ years
2. **AI for documentation** - AI helps explain and organize what I've built
3. **Review all AI output** - I verify everything before publishing
4. **Use AI for tedious tasks** - Documentation, formatting, and organization

## On This Site

Most content on this site is AI-generated:

- Documentation written from my configuration files
- Keybinding references extracted from my configs
- Explanations of how tools work together
- Organization and structure of the site

All content is based on my actual dotfiles and reviewed by me before publishing.

## AI and My Dotfiles

AI now heavily helps me:

- **Create** documentation I never had time to write
- **Debug** configuration issues faster
- **Develop** new features and improvements
- **Organize** years of accumulated configuration

Without AI assistance, this site would not exist. The configurations themselves are the product of years of personal refinement, but the documentation that makes them accessible is AI-enabled.
